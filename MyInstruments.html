My bassoon and contrabassoon

Fairly recently I have bought a new bassoon, a Bell "Amsterdam" model. Buying this instrument would not have been possible without the generous help from donors on my crowdfunding page. If you donated to my crowdfunding and you managed to find this page: many thanks again!

My contrabassoon is built by Kohlert, probably in 1949. The instrument has two serial numbers, but neither of them can be reliably traced. Kohlert relocated in 1949 and started using different serial numbers. Although not sure, it is possible that a second serial number was giving to the instrument do to the relocation of the workshop, which also suggests that the instrument was built in 1949.

For both instruments I make reeds, which are for sale. Please get in touch if you want to place an order.
