I'm

Musician, Engineer, Freelancer

I'm a bassoon player based in Amsterdam and I love to play together. I have a very broad horizon and like to be challenged.

After completing my degree in engineering I decided to follow my heart and persue a carreer as a professional bassoon and contrabassoon player. Since 2019 I study at the Conservatory of Amsterdam in the bassoon studio of Simon Van Holen. I'm a hard worker and try to orientate my as broadly as possible, both musically and intelectually.
